Meet the new postdoc

02/03/2023
Emmanuel Echeverri-Jimenez finished his Ph.D. at North Carolina State University supervised by Dr. Maria Oliver-Hoyo. At NC State, he developed a Virtual Reality Learning Environment to support students' spatial abilities as they extracted, represented, and predicted stereochemical outcomes for the Diels-Alder reaction. At the Balabanoff group, Emmanuel will focus on exploring students' epistemic knowledge in the context of atomic models, and its contrast with corriculum expectations.
