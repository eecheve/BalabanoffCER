Getting the 3D animation to your left
The animation to your left is the reactive coordinate of a gas-phase transition state obtained using Gaussian 16, following a density functional theory method, where instead of considering each electron individually, only electron density is considered.

Exchange-correlation energy functional: This calculation used the m062x Minessota functional, suitable for calculations with Bromine and other heavy main-group elements [1] (although it doesn't account for some London dispersion effects [2]).
Basis Set: Sufficiently large atomic-orbital basis sets are required take advantage of the considerations within energy functionals. Ahlrich's def2-TZVP basis set[3] has been recommended for anionic systems with heavy elements such as the animation in the left.
